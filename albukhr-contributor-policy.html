ALBUKHR

📜 Contributor Policy & Rules

1. Purpose of the Contributor Program

The Albukhr Contributor Program is designed to onboard trusted individuals who have demonstrated knowledge, skills, and commitment to community development, Web3 education, innovation, and ecosystem growth.

2. Eligibility

  • Contributor must provide true and verifiable personal information.
  • Contributor must have relevant skills or experience.
  • Contributor must align with Albukhr’s mission and values.

3. Contributor Responsibilities

  • Act with honesty, transparency, and professionalism.
  • Respect Albukhr’s internal structure and confidentiality.
  • Use Albukhr platforms strictly for approved activities.

4. Prohibited Actions

  • Providing false information or impersonation.
  • Misuse of Albukhr name, tools, or community.
  • Engaging in fraud, scams, or unethical behavior.

5. Enforcement & Sanctions

Albukhr reserves the right to suspend or permanently remove any contributor found violating these rules, without prior notice.

6. Telegram & Internal Access

Access to Albukhr contributor Telegram groups and internal channels is granted only after approval and may be revoked at any time.

By participating in the Albukhr Contributor Program, you agree to abide by all rules and policies stated above.

1. Manufar Shirin Contributors

Shirin Albukhr Contributor an kirkiro shi ne domin shigar da amintattun mutane masu ilimi, kwarewa, da kishin ci gaban al’umma, musamman a fannin Web3, fasaha, da bunkasar ecosystem.

2. Sharuddan Cancanta

  • Dole ne contributor ya bayar da sahihin bayanansa na gaskiya.
  • Dole ne ya mallaki ilimi ko kwarewa da ta dace.
  • Dole ne ya yarda da manufofi da hangen nesan Albukhr.

3. Nauyin da ke Kan Contributor

  • Yin aiki da gaskiya, rikon amana, da ladabi.
  • Girmama sirrin tsari da harkokin cikin gida na Albukhr.
  • Amfani da sunan Albukhr bisa ka’ida kawai.

4. Abubuwan da Aka Haramta

  • Bayar da bayanan ƙarya ko kwaikwayo.
  • Amfani da sunan Albukhr ta haramtacciyar hanya.
  • Yaudara, damfara, ko duk wata muguwar dabi’a.

5. Hukunci da Daukar Mataki

Albukhr na da ikon dakatarwa ko cire contributor daga tsarin gaba ɗaya idan aka same shi da karya doka, ba tare da gargadi ba.

6. Telegram da Izinin Ciki

Shiga rukunin Telegram da tashoshin cikin gida na Albukhr sai bayan amincewa, kuma ana iya janye wannan dama a kowane lokaci.

Da zarar ka shiga shirin Contributor na Albukhr, ka amince da bin dukkan ka’idoji da dokoki da aka bayyana a nan.
