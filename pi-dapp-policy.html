📱 Pi dApp Launch Service

This document outlines the official Policy & Terms of Service for the Albukhr Pi dApp Launch Service.

🎯 Purpose of the Service

The Pi dApp Launch Service is designed to assist individuals and teams who wish to create and launch decentralized applications (dApps) within the Pi Network ecosystem but may lack the technical knowledge, resources, or guidance to do so independently.

🧩 What Albukhr Provides

  • Guidance and assistance in creating a Pi Network dApp
  • Support via Pi Studio or Albukhr Developers
  • Basic technical direction and deployment help
  • Educational guidance on how to manage your own dApp

👤 Ownership & Responsibility

Albukhr does not own any dApp created through this service. All applications are created under the full ownership and responsibility of the user.

Albukhr acts strictly as a service facilitator and advisor.

💳 Fees & Payments

Service fees vary depending on the selected option:

  • Pi Studio Assistance: Lower, flexible service fee
  • Developer-built dApp: Higher professional service fee

Payments cover service effort and technical assistance only. They do not represent investment, partnership, or equity.

🛡️ Review & Approval Process

All submitted requests are reviewed manually by Albukhr administrators. Approval or rejection is based on:

  • Clarity of project idea
  • Feasibility within Pi Network guidelines
  • Compliance with Albukhr ecosystem rules

🚫 Limitations & Disclaimer

Albukhr does not guarantee:

  • Success or adoption of the dApp
  • Financial returns or Pi earnings
  • Approval by the Pi Network Core Team
