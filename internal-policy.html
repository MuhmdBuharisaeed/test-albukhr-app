🏢 ALBUKHR – Internal Project Governance Policy
Internal Use Only

1. Eligibility

Only approved Albukhr contributors with granted internal access may submit internal projects.

2. Ownership Structure

All internal projects remain under Albukhr ecosystem governance. Project founders operate under compliance oversight.

3. Confidentiality

Sensitive data must not be disclosed without executive authorization.

4. Funding & Risk Control

Internal projects are subject to: • Risk classification • Funding stage validation • Escrow governance

5. Misconduct

False submission or misuse of ecosystem authority results in: • Immediate suspension • Account freeze • Permanent ban

6. Approval Process

Every internal submission is reviewed by authorized Albukhr administrators before activation.
